The Electric Book workflow

The Electric Book workflow is a set of processes and tools for making high-quality PDF, epub, website and app versions of books from a single content source. It’s designed for professional book production, and lets team members collaborate on projects remotely.

At its heart is the Electric Book Jekyll template, which you’re looking at now. This template includes:

  • an almost blank book to work into,
  • a book of sample features and typography,
  • a sample translation, and
  • online documentation.

The Electric Book workflow is maintained at Electric Book Works, who also provide paid book-hosting and support plans for publishers.
